Introducing Project Apollo

The fully digital, stand-alone solution to inventory management system by Team Paradox.

The Forecasted Inventory Management System

A software based solution that can bring efficiency to inventory management, and perform inventory management tasks accurately, effortlessly forecast sales to optimize cashflow and increase business.

Excellent Features

Forecasting

Using the ARIMA model products are accurately forecasted to give the latest insights.

Reporting

Extensive reporting throughout the system provides the extra information for growth.

Notifications

Know of immediate changes and alerts through the vast number of notification channels.

Interface

Clean and minimalistic interface allowing for an intuitive experience across the board.

Mobile

Hand-in-hand mobile application for quick access, purchasing tools and board room reporting.

User Management

Manage your staff easily, by defining what their given roles & permissions are in the company.
